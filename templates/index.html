Welcome to Therabalms

About Therabalms

Therabalms is an initiative of Team Monrovia from CWWTech Africa, targeted at providing therapy to individuals with mental health disorders, especially anxiety, depression, stress, and mood swings. We offer self-assessment tests that enable you to appropriately understand the symptoms you are feeling, educational pieces that inform about various disorders, consultation services which provide one-on-one teletherapy, and support groups and communities who offer strength and accountability through your journey to recovery. At Therabalms, your mental health is our priority.

We are here to help you through your recovery process. Connect with us today.
