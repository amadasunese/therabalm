About Therabalms

Welcome to Therabalms

We are here to help you with your mental health.

Therabalms is an initiative of Team Monrovia aimed at providing therapy to individuals with mental health disorders, especially anxiety, depression, stress, and mood swings. Our services include self-assessment tests, educational resources, one-on-one teletherapy, and support groups. Your mental health is our priority.
